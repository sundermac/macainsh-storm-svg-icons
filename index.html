Icon Set Information

Welcome to Orbitz, our icon set is designed to express vibrant energy and playfulness. The icons mainly feature soft, rounded curves and pastel colours. This allows our brand to feel alive and be visually appealing to the children who drink it. We currently offer six flavours: apple, blueberry, mango, orange, watermelon, and peach. Each of our icons uses simple designs of these fruits by sticking to basic shapes and shading. These fruit icons will appear on the label of each of our bottles, along with the dominant colour of the flavour's fruit, making it easy for children to find the flavour they want while keeping the brand fun and modern.
